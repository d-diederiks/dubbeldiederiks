Our Lovestory

almost a decade later and it's just the beginning

It all started in 2015. We were 15 years old and Dams had just recently joined the school choir. I was in the row just in front, and chats soon began to buzz. Ever so subtly, a sweet friendship was beginning to blossom.

In grade 11, a few happy coincidences — God’s plan in action! — led to us dancing together in the school revue competition and later, being on the management team camp together. During both of these, we were inseparable!

Flash forward to grade 12: Dams made sure we had a group of grade 8’s together on the grade 8 camp. Looking back, these days were the very beginning of our lovestory starting to blossom… only we had no idea. I mean, it’s perfectly normal for “just friends” to talk over the phone for hours each night, right?

The most special “aha” moment came on 4 March 2017 — and by 3 April 2017 it was official ❤

What a journey it has been! Two rounds of long distance, rotating between our parents’ houses during Covid, going back to Pretoria, moving to Cape Town, lots of laughs and tears, and the most amazing memories… what a blessing to have grown up together!

🌤️ Weather

October in Robertson is glorious — expect warm sunny days with temperatures around 25–30°C. Evenings in the mountains can get cool, so bring a light layer for after the sun goes down. The braai on Friday will be outdoors, and the ceremony is in the open air, so comfortable shoes on the grass are a good idea.
